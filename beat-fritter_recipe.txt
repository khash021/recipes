>>Ingredients:
	Beats(grated)
	Flour
	Egg
	Spices 
	Jalapeno (experimental)

>>Steps:
	1-Grate your beats and place in a large bowl.
	2-Add your desired spices
	3-Place an egg in the mix.
	4-Add flour.
	5-Work the mixture with hand, slowly adding more flour as needed until desired consistency is achieved.
	6-shape them, and then fry them.
	7-Can use your favorite dip with them.
	8-Enjoy!
